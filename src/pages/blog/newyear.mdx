# 日本の正月を英語で紹介  
*How to Explain Japanese New Year (Shōgatsu)*

## 概要
> Japanese New Year is a family-centered holiday with shrine visits, traditional dishes, and greeting cards.

## 主なトピック
- **初詣** (*first shrine visit*)  
- **おせち料理** (*traditional New Year dishes*)  
- **年賀状** (*New Year greeting cards*)

## 例文
> Many people visit shrines to pray for good fortune and share special dishes with their families.
