# 「おもてなし」を英語で説明する  
*How to Explain "Omotenashi" in English*

> “Omotenashi” means heartfelt hospitality without expecting anything in return.

## Key Points
- Selfless hospitality / Attention to detail / Anticipation  
- Not just service; it’s a cultural mindset

## Example
> In Japan, “omotenashi” is about anticipating guests’ needs and caring with sincerity.
