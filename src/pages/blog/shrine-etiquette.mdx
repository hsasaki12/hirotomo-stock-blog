# 神社参拝マナーを英語で説明する：5つのポイント  
*How to Explain Japanese Shrine Etiquette in English*

## ⛩️ 1. 鳥居をくぐる前に一礼する  
> “Bow lightly before passing through the torii gate. It shows respect to the sacred space.”

## 💧 2. 手水舎で手と口を清める  
> “At the purification fountain, rinse your left hand first, then your right, and finally your mouth.”

## 🏮 3. 本殿の前では二礼二拍手一礼  
> “Bow twice, clap your hands twice, make your wish silently, then bow once more.”

## 🎋 4. お賽銭のマナー  
> “Offer a coin gently — avoid throwing it. The act shows gratitude, not the amount.”

## 🌸 5. 退出時も一礼  
> “When leaving the shrine, bow once again at the torii gate.”
