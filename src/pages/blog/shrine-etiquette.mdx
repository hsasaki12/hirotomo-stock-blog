# 神社参拝マナーを英語で説明する：5つのポイント  
*How to Explain Shrine Etiquette in English*

## 概要
> Japanese shrines are places to show respect to the gods and express gratitude rather than pray for specific wishes.

## 1. 鳥居（Torii Gate）
> The torii gate marks the boundary between the human world and the sacred world.  
Before entering, bow slightly to show respect.

## 2. 手水（Temizu / Purification）
> At the purification basin, rinse your hands and mouth to cleanse your mind and body.

**Steps:**
1. Wash left hand.  
2. Wash right hand.  
3. Rinse mouth (via left hand).  
4. Rinse the handle.

## 3. お賽銭（Offering Money）
> Offer a coin gently — it’s a sign of gratitude, not payment.

## 4. 二礼二拍手一礼
> Bow twice, clap twice, make your wish silently, then bow once more.

## 5. 退出
> When leaving, bow once again toward the main shrine.
