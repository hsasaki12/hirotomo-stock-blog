# 「ごちそうさま」を英語で説明する  
*How to Explain "Gochisosama"*

> “Gochisosama” is said after finishing a meal to express gratitude to those who prepared it and to the food itself.

## 概要
「ごちそうさま」は直訳すると *“It was a feast”*。  
食事を作ってくれた人、食材、命への**感謝の気持ち**を表す言葉です。

> In Japan, people say “Gochisosama deshita” to thank the cook and show respect for the effort behind the meal.

## 英語での表現例
- *Thank you for the meal.*  
- *That was delicious.*  
- *I really enjoyed it.*  

## 文化的な背景
「御馳走」は本来「走り回って食材を集めること」。**手間への感謝**を含みます。

## 例文
> After eating, Japanese people often say “Gochisosama deshita,” meaning “Thank you for the meal.”
