# 「いただきます」を英語で説明しよう
*How to Explain "Itadakimasu" in English*

## 概要
> “Itadakimasu” means “I humbly receive,” expressing gratitude for the food, the people who prepared it, and the life behind the meal.

## 伝え方
- 感謝の対象：料理、作り手、命  
- 直訳ではなく **意味** を説明する

## 例文
> In Japan, we say “itadakimasu” before eating to show gratitude for the food and everyone involved in preparing it.
